Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you. Locally.

Local-First Promise

DuDe Hawaiian is designed as a local-first AI assistant. Your personal data, notes, and health records are stored directly on your own server (The DuDe Machine).

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the operation of the AI agents (Boss, Butler, Librarian, etc.). This includes:

  • Chat logs (stored locally in Vector DB)
  • IoT device states
  • Health metrics you explicitly input
Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. External connections (like LINE Webhook) are used solely to facilitate communication features you enable.
